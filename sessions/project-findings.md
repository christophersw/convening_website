This presentation will introduce the draft output of the Resilient Maryland Defense Communities Project: the **Draft Maryland Defense Communities Collaborative Resiliency Framework**. Chris will walk through the identified gaps and recommendations formulated from the project's interdisciplinary approach which included convening subject matter experts, completing a literature review, hosting resilience exercises, engaging with Mid-Atlantic military installations, as well as conducting surveys and targeted interviews. **Attendees will be invited to provide feedback and make suggestions for improvements.**
