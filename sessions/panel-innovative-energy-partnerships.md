This panel highlights emerging energy resilience and reliability partnerships between military installations, utilities, local governments, and private-sector partners. Panelists will share real-world examples from Maryland and beyond, focusing on how innovative energy projects support mission assurance, grid resilience, and mutual benefits for installations and host communities.
