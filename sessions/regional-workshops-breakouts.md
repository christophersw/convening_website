Participants will break out into regional groups to discuss the unique challenges and opportunities for resilience in their areas. Each group will be facilitated by a subject matter expert who will guide the discussion and help identify key takeaways. Facilitators will guide a discussion on how the identified recommendations from the Draft Maryland Defense Communities Collaborative Resiliency Framework could be applied to their specific regional contexts, and get feedback on how to improve the framework to better meet the needs of different regions.

This session will provide an opportunity for attendees to share their experiences, learn from one another, and develop actionable strategies for improving resilience in their communities.
