Brian M. Lazarchick serves as the Vice President of Electric Operations & Construction for Southern Maryland Electric Cooperative. He directs the resources of the Distribution System Operation section, Fleet Services, Vegetation Management, Contractor Construction, Regional Operations & Construction, and the Construction & Operations Support sections. He is a U.S. Air Force veteran of Operation Enduring Freedom and Operation Iraqi Freedom. In his community, he is a current member of the Owings Architectural Review Committee, board member of CalvertHealth Health Systems Board and a board member of the Charles County Military Alliance Council.
