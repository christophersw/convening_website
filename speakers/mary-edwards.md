Mary Edwards serves as the Chief Credit Officer at Montgomery County Green Bank. Mary brings more than 18 years of commercial credit experience to Montgomery County Green Bank, including formal credit training at BB&T as well as senior credit positions at TD Bank and Burke and Herbert Bank.

Prior to joining Montgomery County Green Bank, Mary served as the Senior Director of Credit Administration at DC Green Bank where she was responsible for the bank's credit policies, procedures, loan operations, and portfolio management. Mary is a long-time resident of Alexandria, VA and has an undergraduate business degree from Salem College.
