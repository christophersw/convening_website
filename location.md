## Getting Here

<div class="panel directions-panel">
  <p>The Campus Center is located on West Lombard Street, between Greene Street and Penn Street. For detailed directions and transportation options, please visit the University of Maryland, Baltimore's official <a href="https://www.umaryland.edu/campuscenter/visit/getting-here/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">"Getting Here" page</a>.</p>

  <h3>By Car</h3>
  <p>Driving directions to the SMC Campus Center, <strong>621 W. Lombard Street, Baltimore, MD 21201</strong>, courtesy of the University of Maryland, Baltimore.</p>

  <h4>From North of Baltimore (outer loop of I-695)</h4>
  <ol>
    <li>Take I-83 South (signs for Baltimore)</li>
    <li>Bear right onto MD-2 S/St. Paul St. (1.2 mi)</li>
    <li>Turn right at E. Lombard St. (0.6 mi)</li>
    <li>Arrive at 621 W. Lombard St.</li>
  </ol>

  <h4>From East of Baltimore (outer loop of I-695)</h4>
  <ol>
    <li>Take I-70 E toward Baltimore</li>
    <li>Take exit toward I-695 S (0.5 mi)</li>
    <li>Keep right at the fork and merge onto I-695 S (5.2 mi)</li>
    <li>Take Exit 11A-11B for I-95 N/I-95 S toward Baltimore/Washington (0.2 mi)</li>
    <li>Keep left at fork and merge onto I-95 N (3.4 mi)</li>
    <li>Take Exit 52 for Russell St. N (0.6 mi)</li>
    <li>Slight left at Baltimore-Washington Pkwy./Russell St. (1.1 mi)</li>
    <li>Continue onto S. Paca St. (0.2 mi)</li>
    <li>Turn left at W. Lombard St. (0.1 mi)</li>
    <li>Arrive at 621 W. Lombard St.</li>
  </ol>

  <h4>From South of Baltimore (Washington, D.C.)</h4>
  <ol>
    <li>Head northeast on Baltimore-Washington Pkwy.</li>
    <li>Continue onto S. Paca St. (0.2 mi)</li>
    <li>Turn left at W. Lombard St. (0.1 mi)</li>
    <li>Arrive at 621 W. Lombard St.</li>
  </ol>

  <h4>From Northeast of Baltimore (outer loop of I-695 toward Philadelphia)</h4>
  <ol>
    <li>Head southwest on I-95 S (will pass through tunnel with a toll)</li>
    <li>Take Exit 53 for I-395 N/MLK Blvd. toward Downtown Baltimore (0.3 mi)</li>
    <li>Merge onto I-395 (1.0 mi)</li>
    <li>Continue onto S. Howard St. (0.2 mi)</li>
    <li>Turn left at W. Lombard St. (0.3 mi)</li>
    <li>Arrive at 621 W. Lombard St.</li>
  </ol>
</div>

## Parking

<div class="panel">
  <p>Parking is available in any <a href="https://www.umaryland.edu/parking/garages/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">University of Maryland, Baltimore garage</a>. The closest garage is the <strong>Pratt Street Garage</strong>, <a href="https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=646+W+Pratt+St%2C+Baltimore%2C+MD+21201" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">646 W Pratt St.</a>, which is connected to the SMC Campus Center. Attendees can access the SMC Campus Center from Level 3 on the east side of the garage. The Penn Street Garage, <a href="https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=120+S+Penn+St%2C+Baltimore%2C+MD+21201" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">120 S Penn St.</a>, is one block away. </p>

  <p> <strong>Attendees will receive a parking voucher at the registration table upon check-in.</strong> The voucher can be used in any UMB garage. </p>
</div>
